Our Story

A Firm Built on Principle

Founded in 2010 by Advocate Thabo Matshona, our firm was established with a clear vision: to provide accessible, high-quality legal services to the people of Pretoria and Gauteng.

What began as a small practice has grown into a respected full-service law firm, serving individuals, families, and businesses across South Africa. Our growth has been driven by one unwavering principle — putting our clients first.

Today, Matshona Attorneys is recognised for our expertise across multiple areas of law, our commitment to justice, and our track record of delivering results. We combine the personal attention of a boutique firm with the capability and resources of a larger practice.
